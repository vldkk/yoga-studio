Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is an active, physically difficult practice of breath and movement synchronization to generate an inner heat produced to clean the body. This form of yoga is a perfect way to build strength and tone the whole organism. Get ready to perspire profusely because of quick movements.

INSTRUCTOR: ANN DOHERTY

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa Yoga is a vigorous class consisting of asana practice to strenghten body and mind. Vinyasa means "connection of movement and breath". Through a creative series of asanas the teacher prepares you for more challenging poses.

JOIN GROUP

INSTRUCTOR: ANN DOHERTY

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is an ancient system, the purpose of which is to reach the heights of human development through special body-oriented practices and knowledge about the human body. Hatha yoga begins with the body — with its feeling, experiencing, and accepting different sensation.

Meditation

Numerous scientific researches show the advantages of meditation on the emotional, physical, and mental levels. Daily meditations show the improved functioning of the nervous system, physical changes in the brain, enhanced creativity, and greater overall health.

Yin Yoga

Yin is one of the yoga styles which joins together all the aspects of Hatha yoga, Taoist philosophy, the Chinese medicine's conception of the meridians, and Buddhist methods of meditation. Yin yoga classes positively influence the whole body, inside and out.

Prenatal Yoga

Prenatal Yoga is a special yoga practice designed for expectant women. This style can help would-be-mothers to get in touch with their babies emotionally and physically. Prenatal Yoga will assist them in becoming more flexible during the pregnancy and after the delivery.
